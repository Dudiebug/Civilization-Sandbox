# Story Director Authority

## Allowed
Rank eligible incident candidates, adjust timing within declared bounds, suppress repetition, protect configured recovery windows, prefer connected event chains, highlight neglected actors/systems, choose notification prominence, and frame factual chronicle output.

## Forbidden
Bypass preconditions, create resources/actors/knowledge, predetermine battle or political outcomes, ignore logistics/law/geography, target because the camera watches, violate deterministic replay, or exceed world/profile limits.

## Verification
Pure Simulation is the reference. Chronicler/Adversarial/Chaotic profiles may change selection and presentation only within simulation-valid opportunity sets. Every selection emits a decision trace and delegates resolution to the owning domain.
