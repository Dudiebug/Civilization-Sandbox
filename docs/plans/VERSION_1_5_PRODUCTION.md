# Version 1.5 Production Coverage - Complete Early Modern

This preserves the original Blueprint v2.0 Version 1.0 completeness target under the new Version 1.5 label. Counts remain evidence-governed targets, but this is the full early-modern horizon.

## World and time
- [ ] Bounded 2.5D Small/Standard/Large worlds, terrain, hydrology, climate variability, resources, deposits, and hazards.
- [ ] Typed time, fixed scheduling, speed controls, backlog reporting, and camera independence.

## Persistent life
- [ ] 10,000 stable people through births, deaths, save/load, and long-run compaction.
- [ ] Households, dependents, inheritance, possessions, migration, and knowledge-carrier continuity.
- [ ] Needs, health, work, skills, education, traits, attitudes, beliefs, ties, and inspectable decisions.

## Physical settlements
- [ ] Autonomous founding and survival.
- [ ] Parcels, rights, buildings, construction, damage, repair, abandonment, salvage, and ruins.
- [ ] Society-dependent roads, paths, bridges, neighborhoods, services, and redevelopment.

## Economy and logistics
- [ ] 18 commodity classes, conservation, production, labor, ownership, prices, simple credit, and failure.
- [ ] Real stockpiles, reservations, shipments, manifests, routes, delays, loss, and bottlenecks.

## Civilization and divergence
- [ ] Institutions, four government presets, policy/enforcement, legitimacy, factions, reform, coups, and rebellion.
- [ ] Culture, religion, patriotism, identity, assimilation, extinction, and revival.
- [ ] Capability network, invention, adoption, diffusion, loss, reverse engineering, and six domains.
- [ ] Civilization formation, strategic planning, merger, fracture, collapse, successor states, and restoration.

## International and conflict
- [ ] Believed information, 12 diplomatic actions, executable treaties, trade, alliances, coercion, and peace.
- [ ] Persistent soldiers, supply, physical sector conflict, occupation, casualties, refugees, damage, and rebuilding.

## Environment and history
- [ ] Pollution, renewable/finite resources, ecosystems, disasters, extinction, recovery, and player restoration.
- [ ] Persistent world-defining ruins and scars through centuries and compaction.
- [ ] Historical event graph, interventions, chronicles, timelines, maps, and perspective records.
- [ ] Story Director that selects/paces valid candidates without changing simulation truth.

## Player and interface
- [ ] Full omniscient inspection plus selected-actor knowledge perspectives.
- [ ] 13 primary workspace target, causal traces, search, comparison, overlays, alerts, tracking, and history.
- [ ] 24 first-playable power target with distinct Binding Order, indirect intervention, and Force Outcome semantics.
- [ ] Accessibility baseline and non-color information encoding.

## Product quality
- [ ] Determinism, tests, traces, performance, saves, migrations, diagnostics, documentation, and evidence.
- [ ] Blueprint reference budgets, 24-hour soak, 500-year run, and 100-year replay.
- [ ] Creator-visible acceptance and release process.
