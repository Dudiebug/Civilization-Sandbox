# Prompt - Build 08: God Sandbox

Complete the defining player loop: omniscient observation, causal inspection, Binding Orders, indirect intervention, Force Outcome, and persistent history.

Player outcome: I can search and inspect the implemented world, understand important choices and failures, issue a representative high-level order, alter a condition and let the simulation respond, force a result that ordinary actors could not achieve, and compare the consequences afterward.

Use current integrated systems to select a small but representative power set. Plan 4-8 vertical slices covering search/inspection, overlays, causal explanations, one or more Binding Orders, indirect material/environmental intervention, Force Outcome preview/validation/reconciliation, intervention ancestry, history, and accessible consolidated UI.

Keep the three authority paths visibly distinct. UI never directly mutates simulation state. Force Outcome declares sources/sinks, validates references, reconciles dependent state transactionally, and records that the player caused it. Story/history may frame facts but never fabricate outcomes.

Acceptance: the same world can branch through observation, direction, intervention, and force; the player can inspect why branches differ; save/reload and history preserve every consequential intervention.

Do not implement all twenty-four powers, thirteen final workspaces, final art, or full Story Director profiles before Version 1.5.
