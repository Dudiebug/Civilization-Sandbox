# Prompt - Build 03: Living Camp

Turn the Founding Camp into a persistent human-scale simulation without losing the simple playable loop.

Player outcome: named people persist across days and seasons; lightweight households or support groups share obligations; people work according to needs and simple aptitudes; illness, injury, arrival, migration, incapacity, and death affect the camp; structures progress physically and can be abandoned or damaged; a factual timeline records major events.

Inspect playtest bottlenecks first. Propose 4-8 vertical slices that improve the felt life of the camp. Add only fields that a visible behavior, explanation, persistence rule, or near-term extension requires. Keep traits behaviorally active and bounded rather than decorative biographies.

Preserve stable identity, explicit time, deterministic decisions, resource conservation, construction attribution, and save compatibility. Use factual event records linked to people, places, and outcomes. Add focused regression scenarios for any failure discovered during play.

Acceptance: I can follow one named person from need through work, movement, construction, relationship/support responsibility, injury, migration, or death and understand the reasons; major events survive save/reload; the camp remains playable at speed.

Do not add exhaustive genealogy, romance simulation, large speech libraries, institutions, full markets, broad politics, diplomacy, or war.
