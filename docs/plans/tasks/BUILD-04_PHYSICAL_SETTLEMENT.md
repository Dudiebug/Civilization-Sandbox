# Prompt - Build 04: Physical Settlement

Make a Living Camp autonomously grow into a durable physical village through actual labor, materials, production, routes, damage, and rebuilding.

Player outcome: people choose and execute projects, specialize into a bounded set of occupations, operate simple workplaces, haul materials, construct several useful building types, form paths/roads, experience service or route bottlenecks, repair damage, salvage abandoned structures, and leave persistent ruins or scars.

Use creator play feedback to select a deliberately small resource, recipe, occupation, building, and service envelope. Propose 4-8 vertical slices, each ending in something visible in Unity. Do not prebuild the full Version 1.5 catalogs.

All goods must have source, owner/location, reservation, movement, and destination. Projects require real material and labor progress. Ordinary exact placement remains autonomous; the player may direct project intent, not place every routine object.

Acceptance: disrupting a key route or building causes understandable shortages or displacement; the settlement adapts, repairs, replaces, or declines; recovery uses real resources and labor; a lasting physical/history record remains.

Do not add multiple civilizations, deep prices/credit, broad institutions, full politics, diplomacy, war, or final visuals.
