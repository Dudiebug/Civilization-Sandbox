# Milestone 0.6 - Connected Region

**Status:** Planned; task contracts intentionally deferred until Milestone 0.5 passes.
**Detailed program context:** `PRE_1_0_ROADMAP.md`, Section 10.

## Purpose
Connect accepted societies through physical routes, shipments, exchange, shortages, agreements, and migration.

## Entry conditions
- At least two society configurations pass the divergence gate.
- Resource location, inventories, route capacity, settlement response, and save/replay are stable.
- D07 selects the minimum exchange abstraction needed for legible dependence.

## Player-visible result
One society can depend on another for a needed good. Shipments traverse real routes. Damage or restriction at a bridge/corridor produces delayed, location-specific shortages, changed priorities, migration, and attempts to repair, replace, negotiate, or adapt.

## Workstreams
1. Contact, recognition, communication reach, sparse relevant relationships, and minimum believed-information state.
2. Stable shipments/manifests, origin/destination inventories, corridor reuse, travel delay, capacity, and failure.
3. Approved thin exchange/allocation model; simple prices only if D07 justifies them.
4. Executable basic agreements and restrictions required by the gate scenario.
5. Persistent individual/household migration with bounded destination evaluation.
6. Route, shipment, stockpile, shortage/surplus, contact, and migration views.
7. Integrated bridge/corridor causal history and downstream project/relationship responses.

## Integrated gate scenario
Societies become materially interdependent through a critical corridor. Interrupt it. Shipments stop or reroute, local stocks change after travel delay, societies respond differently, and migration/projects/agreements or coercive pressure follow. Goods do not teleport, duplicate, or vanish without declared cause.

## Required gate evidence
- shipment conservation and route-state integrity;
- route failure/detour and bounded cache invalidation;
- exchange/allocation stability at approved depth;
- migration membership/home/work updates;
- agreement obligations and breach handling;
- integrated causal-chain timeline and player explanation;
- concurrent shipment/relationship performance;
- independent review and creator acceptance.

## Decisions opened
- D07 trade, allocation, price, and route complexity.

## Decisions intentionally left open
Complex markets/credit, full treaties, alliances at depth, espionage, warfare, occupation, global logistics, final UI/power roster, and Story Director.

## Stop or redesign triggers
- Exchange fabricates local availability or ignores travel time.
- Per-shipment or per-person routing becomes a global search sink.
- Relationship AI uses omniscient truth.
- Shortages are only aggregate modifiers with no physical source.

## Handoff to 0.7
Deliver a connected regional civilian system in which supply, routes, work, households, migration, and relationships can make conflict consequential.
