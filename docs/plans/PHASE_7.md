# Milestone 0.8 - God Sandbox

**Status:** Planned; task contracts intentionally deferred until Milestone 0.7 passes.
**Detailed program context:** `PRE_1_0_ROADMAP.md`, Section 12.

## Purpose
Complete the defining player interaction loop over the implemented regional simulation.

## Entry conditions
- Conflict and recovery pass without hidden player support.
- Important decisions/events already emit stable factual evidence.
- D09 is resolved from integrated usability tests, not from the full Version 1.5 catalogs.

## Player-visible result
The player can observe, search, inspect causes, issue Binding Orders, alter material/social conditions, invoke explicit Force Outcomes, compare consequences, and follow intervention ancestry through history and physical scars.

## Workstreams
1. Omniscient search, selection, follow/tracking, and core overlays over implemented categories.
2. Consolidated inspectors with one-line causes and approved factor depth.
3. Representative Binding Orders over implemented settlement, exchange, relationship, migration, and conflict decisions.
4. Representative indirect interventions that propagate through ordinary systems.
5. Force Outcomes with preview, validation, reconciliation, aftermath, and permanent intervention records.
6. Undo as compensating command or branch; no silent history deletion.
7. Factual timeline, event links, and protected major ruins.
8. Consolidated UI, keyboard/input abstraction, scalable text, non-color encoding, reduced effects, and pause-and-inspect.

## Integrated gate scenario
Use one crisis three ways: Binding Order, indirect intervention, and Force Outcome. Show different authority, feasibility, implementation, blockers, mutations, consequences, and history. Save/load and branch comparison remain coherent.

## Required gate evidence
- command-class authority and validation;
- Force Outcome reconciliation/invariants;
- intervention ancestry and undo/branch behavior;
- explanation coverage and source links;
- search/overlay/trace/history performance;
- UI task-completion and accessibility review;
- independent review and creator acceptance.

## Decisions opened
- D09 exact Version 1.0 power roster, UI organization, and explanation depth.

## Decisions intentionally left open
Full 24-power set, 13-workspace target, every overlay, full belief-comparison UI, Story Director profiles, historical maps, large chronicle library, and post-1.0 allocation.

## Stop or redesign triggers
- Binding Orders become disguised direct mutation or micromanagement.
- Force Outcomes bypass data integrity or erase aftermath.
- Omniscient UI leaks ground truth into AI knowledge.
- UI breadth makes the core loop inaccessible.
- History cannot distinguish natural, ordered, intervened, and forced causes.

## Handoff to 0.9
Deliver the complete lean gameplay loop and a measured release-critical defect/risk list, without treating Version 1.5 breadth as a hidden requirement.
