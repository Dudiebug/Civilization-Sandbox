# Deferred Work and Non-Goals

## Never core
- Live LLM citizens or governments.
- One giant civilization brain.
- Manual placement of ordinary buildings, roads, lots, zones, or districts.
- Fully rendered ordinary interiors or room-by-room skyscrapers.
- Universal linear technology tree.
- Camera-dependent authoritative simulation.
- Scripted off-screen outcomes.
- Black-box ML for authoritative civilization behavior.
- Spherical core world.
- Realistic anatomical gore.

## Deferred beyond Version 1.0
Unless a later creator decision promotes a tightly bounded item, Version 1.0 does not require:
- the 10,000-person release gate or all large world presets;
- the full 18-resource, 16-building, and 16-occupation catalog;
- full inheritance, personality evolution, religion, factions, coups, or extensive social ties;
- detailed prices, credit, debt, multiple property regimes, or deep market simulation;
- the complete capability hypergraph, reverse engineering, and six-domain breadth;
- the full 12-action diplomacy set, complex treaties, occupation, insurgency, or extensive warfare doctrine;
- full ecosystems, biological extinction, pollution, or broad disaster simulation;
- Story Director profiles, historical maps, perspective historiography, or long-form chronicles;
- 24 powers and 13 separate UI workspaces;
- multiplayer, executable mods, console certification, cloud saves, final localization breadth, final soundtrack, or marketing telemetry.

## Planned early-modern depth horizon
Versions 1.1-1.4 select bounded parts of the deferred list. Version 1.5 completes the original Blueprint early-modern target. Working release themes are planning aids, not commitments.

## Later ages
Industrialization, electricity, automobiles, aircraft, nuclear systems, computing, space, and multi-planet systems begin at Version 2.0 or later.

## First-six-month prohibition
Do not build later-age content, nuclear weapons, skyscrapers, modern vehicles, detailed navies, advanced constitutions, large speech libraries, final character art, interiors, multiplayer, procedural planets, or a universal technology tree. Do not prebuild Version 1.1-1.5 systems merely because their interfaces are visible.
