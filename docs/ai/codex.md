# AI-System Scope Instructions

This directory governs authoritative coded intelligence.

- No live language model may decide citizen, government, economy, diplomacy, culture, technology, or war outcomes.
- Every consequential decision requires a versioned behavior contract, bounded candidates, legal preconditions, observer-specific knowledge, deterministic selection, structured trace, scenario tests, and a performance budget.
- Utility scoring selects among legal possibilities; it never grants authority, knowledge, resources, or feasibility.
- Avoid a generic universal evaluator. Reuse canonical primitives through role- and state-specific contracts.
- Do not tune weights until fixtures and traces reveal a contract-level reason.
- A behavior change requires contract versioning, before/after evidence, regression review, and save/replay consideration.
