# AI Decision Laboratory

## Purpose
Prove the common decision runtime before broad person, settlement, or government behavior is built.

## Required laboratory capabilities
- Declarative behavior-contract catalog with stable IDs and versions.
- Scenario fixture loader and deterministic headless runner.
- Candidate/precondition/observation/score/selection trace capture.
- Property, metamorphic, and multi-seed statistical testing.
- Before/after trace and distribution comparison.
- Save/load during active commitments.
- Batch performance and allocation measurement.
- Deliberate-defect injection to prove the harness detects failures.

## First three decisions
1. Founding-site selection.
2. Urgent personal-needs action.
3. Settlement labor allocation.

## Gate
Do not proceed to broad AI until all three pass deterministic scenarios, invariants, metamorphic tests, multi-seed diversity, save/replay, canonical trace comparison, 10,000-person batch budgets, independent review, and at least ten deliberately seeded defect detections.

Failure means redesign primitives and fixtures, not merely tune more weights.
