# Decision Authority Matrix

| Layer | Owns | May constrain | Must not directly do | Activation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Alliance/international | collective security, bloc commitments, mediation | civilization diplomatic posture | move people, transfer inventory | Version 1.5 horizon |
| Civilization | strategy, diplomacy, mobilization, major programs | government and regional priorities | choose individual footsteps or purchases | Later approved pre-1.0 milestone |
| Government | law, tax, budget, policing, authorization, succession | settlements, institutions, workplaces | mutate personal needs or route progress | Later approved pre-1.0 milestone |
| Region | inter-settlement infrastructure and claims | settlement projects and access | construct without projects/resources | Later approved pre-1.0 milestone |
| Settlement | land use, services, local projects | workplaces and households through law/access | teleport labor or goods | First contract in Milestone 0.2 |
| Institution/workplace | education, faith, research, production, hiring | members/employees within rules | declare national war | Labor proof only; broader scope reserved |
| Household | housing, pooled resources, dependents, migration | member shared commitments | override emergency individual action | Lean subset in Milestone 0.3 |
| Person | needs, goals, work, movement, attitudes | own commitments | make government/civilization decisions | First contract in Milestone 0.2 |
| Story Director | eligible incident selection, pacing, framing | notification and candidate timing within bounds | fabricate actors, resources, knowledge, or outcomes | Full runtime reserved for Version 1.5 horizon |
| Player Binding Order | decision-level obligation | owning layer's priority | bypass implementation feasibility | Later approved pre-1.0 milestone |
| Player Force Outcome | validated world-state mutation | reconciliation aftermath | bypass data integrity or history record | Later approved pre-1.0 milestone |

Public APIs should make forbidden cross-layer calls difficult or impossible. An Activation entry is a planning boundary, not evidence that the layer is implemented.
