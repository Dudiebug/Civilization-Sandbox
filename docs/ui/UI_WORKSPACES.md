# UI Capability Map

## Version 1.0 rule
Version 1.0 must expose the required information and commands, but it may consolidate them into fewer screens or panels. The exact workspace count and navigation model are decided during Milestone 0.8 after the simulation and player powers are observable.

Required capability families:
- world creation/rules, HUD, time, alerts, and tracking;
- contextual inspection for people, households, buildings, settlements, and societies;
- resource, route, trade, diplomacy, conflict, and construction information appropriate to implemented systems;
- causal explanation, major history, player orders, interventions, Force Outcome previews, and intervention log;
- accessible search, focus, labels, units, data age, and observer perspective.

## Version 1.5 horizon
The Blueprint's 13 primary workspace target remains:
1. World creation and rules.
2. Main HUD, time, alerts, and bookmarks.
3. Person inspector.
4. Household/home inspector.
5. Settlement/city inspector.
6. Workplace/institution/building inspector.
7. Civilization/government inspector.
8. Technology/capability portfolio.
9. Economy, inventories, prices, and logistics.
10. Diplomacy and treaty workspace.
11. War and operations workspace.
12. History, ruins, extinction, and comparison.
13. Player powers, Binding Orders, Force Outcome, and intervention log.

Each implemented view reads immutable query models and sends validated commands.
