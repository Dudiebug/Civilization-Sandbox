# Roadmap Rebaseline Summary

**Revision date:** 2026-07-20
**Planning status:** Creator-approved release-scope rebaseline; no game implementation has begun.

## Decision
The original Blueprint v2.0 early-modern Version 1.0 target is retained as the north-star **Version 1.5** target. The first commercial Version 1.0 becomes a smaller regional civilization sandbox with the same identity and a complete causal loop.

## What changed
- The pre-1.0 roadmap is now Milestones **0.1 through 0.9**.
- The first 25 task contracts now cover Milestones **0.1 through 0.3**, ending at the Living Camp gate.
- Later task contracts are created only after the previous milestone passes, preventing premature detail and unnecessary creator decisions.
- The fixed 10,000-person, 24-power, 13-workspace, and full historical-system targets no longer block Version 1.0. They remain Version 1.5 horizon targets unless later evidence and creator decisions change them.
- Exact world size, population target, content counts, government models, economy depth, combat depth, power roster, and release test duration remain deliberately undecided until their milestone decision gates.

## What did not change
- Persistent named people and camera-independent causality.
- Physical construction, roads, damage, rebuilding, ruins, and historical continuity.
- Society-dependent divergence rather than scripted faction identities.
- Autonomous exact implementation rather than ordinary manual placement.
- Omniscient inspection and explainable coded intelligence.
- Separate Binding Order, indirect intervention, and Force Outcome semantics.
- Semantic saves, stable identities, bounded work, and evidence-gated development.
- Industrialization remains Version 2.0 or later.

## Authority
`docs/decisions/ADR-001_RELEASE_SCOPE_REBASELINE.md` governs release labels and roadmap scope where it conflicts with Blueprint v2.0. The PDF remains authoritative for the north-star identity, architecture, and Version 1.5 completeness target.
